The Wakami Wailers have been performing their unique brand of cultural interpretation for over 30 years. Through their music, humour, and stories, they transport audiences back to simpler times, when Canada was a young developing country occupied by trappers, minors, settlers, and lumberjacks.

Music

Three award winning albums have been recorded by the Wakami Wailers, in addition to the soundtracks for three films. You can listen to samples of their music online, and their albums are available to purchase as CDs or downloadable in MP3 format from iTunes.
